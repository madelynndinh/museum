About this Artifact

This ceremonial mask from the Senufo people of West Africa dates to the 15th century. It was used in important community rituals and represents the connection between the physical and spirit worlds. The mask features intricate carving techniques and shows evidence of multiple paint layers, suggesting it was used over generations.

Rotate, zoom, and explore the 3D model to discover annotation points that reveal details about the mask's construction, symbolism, and conservation history.
